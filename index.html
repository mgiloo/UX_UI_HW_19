    • Hello! I'm Margaret Allotey-Pappoe

    I believe in crafting meaningful digital experiences for users through human-centered designs.

    I am a designer who loves color, typography, and images. My passion lies in learning and finding meaningful solutions to problems. People are at the forefront of the work I do. Great experiences are made when they stem from well-crafted products. My goal is to make products, that motivates users and are easy to understand.

    UX SKILLS
    USER RESEARCH

    User research is done to help me understand the problem and pain points of users. I conduct 1-on-1 interviews and surveys as part of the design process to clarify and have a deeper understanding of the problem from users to help find the best solution. I create user personas, empathy and journey maps, competitor research, usability testing, A/B and accessibility testing.

    USER INTERFACE DESIGN

    My skill set in user interface design includes creating user flows, information architecture (IA) organizing and structuring content, card sorting, wireframes, prototyping—paper, and mid to high-fidelity using Figma & usability testing. I also have a keen eye for visual design—typography, color theory, layout, and design history and theory.

    WEB DEVELOPMENT

    I have HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT & JQUERY skills when it comes to front-end design. I use these skills to build responsive web interfaces that will appeal to the users.
